Welcome!

You are a low-income college senior in California preparing to graduate in a few weeks. Between internships, classes, money, friendships, and burnout, it feels impossible to balance everything at once. You’ve recently started thinking more seriously about how your current choices might shape your future after college.

A friend recommended this interactive experience to help think about your current priorities in life. Explore the experience and respond naturally to the situations you are given.

During testing, try to:

  • Complete the decision quiz honestly and intuitively
  • Explore how your choices affect your future outcome

As you explore, think about:

  • What feels meaningful, confusing, or emotionally impactful
  • Whether the results feel believable or fair
  • Whether the experience makes you reflect on your own priorities or trade-offs

There are no right or wrong answers. The goal is to explore how small decisions can shape different futures.
