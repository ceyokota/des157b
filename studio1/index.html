Wish I were a squirrel,

either a boy or a girl.

Always so resilient,

never have to be subservient.

Surely I'd survive the cold,

acorns galore my pot of gold.

Nice and cozy in someone's attic,

don't even need a darn schematic.

No taxes or electric bill,

just relaxing and taking my fill.

It's just fun to be an acrobat,

limb to limb with a little chitchat.

Jury duty's out of the question,

that would only give me indigestion.

No car insurance, no worldly care;

never even have to apply for welfare.

Wish I were a squirrel,

either a boy or a girl.

Always so resilient,

never have to be subservient.
